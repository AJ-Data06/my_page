Humidity

Humidity varies among cities even at the same Latitude. There are more than just latitude affecting the humidity (i.e. geographic location). However, we did saw that the humidity among cities around equator retain relatively moderate-to-high-humidity (40%-100%). This could mainly cause by the constant hot weather around equator area.
